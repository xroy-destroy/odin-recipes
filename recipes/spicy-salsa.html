Spicy Salsa

Kick It Up!

Salsa, the topping you deserve and the topping that belongs on darn near everything. Tacos, enchiladas, burritos, chips, and the list goes on! It's always whats missing from your plate, that little extra kick needed to make for a memorable dish.

Ingredients

  • Chopped tomatoes, 1 1/2 cups
  • Chopped onion, 1/2 cup
  • Fresh chopped cilantro, 1/2 cup
  • Fresh garlic, 3 cloves
  • Grilled tomatillo, medium
  • Grilled jalapeño, large
  • Fresh-squeezed lime juice, 2 large limes
  • Salt, to taste

Steps

  1. Set your favorite grill, or open flame source, on high to grill the jalepeño and tomatatillo
  2. Use tongs to carefully place the vegetables just above the flame
  3. Look for the vegetables skin to turn black and blistered, then turn until all sides are thoroughly grilled - avoid ashy white
  4. Set aside grilled vegetables to cool for a minimum of 20 minutes
  5. Load up the blender with the garlic, jalepeño, tomatillo, and cilantro
  6. Blend, but use the pulse setting for about 30 seconds
  7. Add the rest of the ingredients; salt, lime juice, onion, and tomato
  8. Blend until you like what you see
  9. Enjoy!
