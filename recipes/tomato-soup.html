Roasted Tomato Soup

A Savoury Taste Of Home

Judy Garland said it best, "There's no place like home." A roasted tomato soup with a slice of warm sourdough is a meal for the soul and a home away from home.

Ingredients

  • Chopped tomatoes, 1 1/4 lbs
  • Chopped yellow onion, 1/2 cup
  • Fresh garlic, 3 cloves
  • High quality extra virgin live oil, 1 tablespoon
  • Fresh ground pepper
  • Salt, to taste
  • Chopped parsely, 1 tablespoon
  • Low sodium broth, 1 1/2 cups
  • Tomato paste, 1 tablespoon
  • Heavy cream, 1/4 cup
  • Fresh garnishes of oregano and basil
  • Your favorite fresh-baked bread

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°
  2. Chop tomatoes and discard seeds
  3. Spread tomatoes over a rimmed backing sheet
  4. Drizzle with olive oil, sprinkle with salt and parsely, and add a generous amount of pepper
  5. Toss mixture in pan by hand
  6. Tuck the garlic cloves within the tomatoes to avoid burning
  7. Roast for 30-40 minutes, and set aside to cool
  8. In a sauce pan, warm broth and stir in tomato paste until dissolved and then simmer for 5-10 minutes
  9. Allow to paste and broth mixture cool
  10. Add cooled roasted tomatoes and broth paste mix to a blender
  11. Blend until satisfied
  12. Add cream, pepper, and salt to taste
  13. Add any desired garnish
  14. Enjoy with fresh bread
